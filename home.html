Features

Historical sales Data

The most important feature for sales prediction is historical sales data.
The ML algorithm can be trained on this data to identify patterns and trends in sales over time.

Market trends and seasonality

Incorporating market trends and seasonality can help improve the accuracy of sales prediction.
For example, sales of winter clothing are likely to be higher in winter months than in summer months

Economic indicators

Economic indicators such as GDP, inflation, and interest rates can impact sales.
By incorporating these indicators into sales prediction using ML, businesses can adjust their sales strategies accordingly.

Product features

Product features such as price, quality, and availability can impact sales.
By analyzing these features using ML algorithms, businesses can identify which products are likely to sell better in specific markets.

Customer behavior

Analyzing customer behavior such as purchase history, demographics, and preferences can provide valuable insights for sales prediction.
This can help identify customer segments with high sales potential and optimize marketing strategies to target them effectively.
